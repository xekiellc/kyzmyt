How verification works

Three layers. No exceptions.

Before anyone appears in your feed, they've cleared all three. Every time. No shortcuts.

1
Government ID verification

You upload a government-issued photo ID. We confirm it's real, not expired, and not altered. Then you take a live selfie — your face must match your ID. Not a photo from five years ago. Your face, today.

✓ Liveness detection included
Takes ~2–3 minutes No credit check Does not affect your credit score Used only for identity verification
2
Criminal background screening

A real background check. Sex offender registry. Felony history. Misdemeanor history. Results go directly to us — not through the applicant. You cannot alter, hide, or manipulate them.

✓ Results sent directly to Kyzmyt
Never shared with other members Used only for safety filtering
3
AI-assisted fraud detection + human review

Our system analyzes profile photos for signs of AI generation and flags behavioral patterns that suggest bots or fake accounts. Every flag goes to a human reviewer. No account is removed — or approved — without human eyes on it.

✓ Human review on every flagged account
