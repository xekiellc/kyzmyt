Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026
We take privacy seriously. We do not sell your personal data. We never share your verification documents. We keep your data only as long as necessary.

What we collect

We collect the information you provide when creating your profile: name, date of birth, email, photos, and your responses to our values and personality questionnaires. We also collect usage data — which profiles you view, how you interact with the platform.

Verification data

Identity verification is processed entirely by Stripe Identity. We receive only a verified/not verified result. We do not receive or store copies of your government ID or selfie photos.

Background checks are processed by Checkr. We receive a status result (clear, consider, etc.) and store it in our database. We do not receive the full report detail unless required by law.

Financial verification is processed by Plaid. We receive only a confirmed verification badge — we do not store your account numbers, balances, or transaction history.

How we use your data

Your profile data is used to show your profile to compatible members and to calculate compatibility scores. Your contact information is used to send transactional emails and safety check-in messages. We use aggregate, anonymized data to improve our matching algorithms.

What we never do

We never sell your personal data to third parties. We never show advertising on the platform. We never share your profile with anyone outside the platform without your consent or a legal requirement.

Data retention

If you delete your account, your profile and all associated data is deleted within 30 days. Verification records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal compliance purposes. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained indefinitely.

Your rights

You may request a copy of your data, correction of inaccurate data, or deletion of your account at any time by contacting privacy@kyzmyt.com. Ohio residents have additional rights under applicable state law.

Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication and analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4) to understand platform usage. You may opt out of analytics cookies at any time.

Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@kyzmyt.com
