Safety isn't a feature. It's the foundation.

Every decision we make starts with safety. Here's exactly how we protect our members.

🪪
Identity verification — everyone, no exceptions
Every Kyzmyt member completes government ID verification and a liveness selfie before they can interact with the platform. This is powered by Stripe Identity — the same technology used by financial institutions. You cannot join without it. Period.
What we check
Government-issued ID (driver's license or passport) + real-time selfie comparison. Stripe's AI confirms the photo matches and the document is genuine. Results are sent directly to our database — users cannot interfere with the result.
🔍
Criminal background checks — tamper-proof by design
Every member pays for and passes a real criminal background check powered by Checkr. We check national criminal records, the sex offender registry in all 50 states, felony and misdemeanor history, and the global watchlist.
Why it's tamper-proof
Results travel directly from Checkr's servers to our database via a secure, signed webhook. No user or admin can alter the result. A background check badge on a profile means Checkr confirmed clear — not that someone claimed they were clear.
📍
Date check-in — an industry first
Before any first date, members can set a safety check-in time and designate a trusted contact. At the check-in time, we text the member. If they don't respond within 15 minutes, we automatically alert their trusted contact with a message and their last known location. This feature is free for all verified members.
🚨
Human-reviewed reports within 24 hours
Every report submitted on our platform is reviewed by a human moderator within 24 hours. We take reports seriously. Bans are permanent. We do not give bad actors second chances.
🤖
AI photo verification
Profile photos are analyzed for deepfake indicators and catfish patterns. Photos that fail verification do not appear on the platform. Your main photo is also compared against your ID selfie for consistency.
🔇
Block and report — one tap, no questions
You can block any member instantly from any conversation. Blocked users cannot see your profile or contact you in any way. Reports go directly to our moderation queue with no visible indication to the reported person that a report was filed.
Safe by design.
Not by promise.
We built Kyzmyt because people deserve to know who they're meeting. If you have safety concerns about a member or an interaction, contact us immediately.
