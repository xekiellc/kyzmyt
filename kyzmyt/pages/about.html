"Fate; destiny; the inevitable unfolding of events written before we arrived."

From the Turkish kısmet, from Arabic qisma (قِسْمَة) — meaning "portion" or "lot." The idea that some things are simply meant to happen. We took that word, made it ours, and gave it a distinctive spelling that belongs to no one else. Because if fate is real, it deserves its own name.

Why We Exist

The Problem with Modern Dating

Online dating was supposed to make connection easier. In some ways, it did. But it also created an environment where nobody knows who anyone really is. Where a profile photo might be five years old — or belong to someone else entirely. Where anyone can say anything, and there's no way to verify a word of it.

The result? Millions of people swiping through strangers with no real trust, no real safety, and no real accountability. People who've been burned before approach every new connection with anxiety instead of excitement. Legitimate people are treated with suspicion they didn't earn.

What if you could walk into every first date knowing the person across from you is exactly who they said they were?

That's the question Kyzmyt was built to answer. Not with a better algorithm. Not with a new swipe mechanic. With actual verification.

Our Approach

The World's First Verified Dating Platform

Kyzmyt requires every member — every single one — to complete a government ID verification and a full national criminal background check before they can interact with anyone on the platform. Not optional. Not a premium feature. Mandatory for everyone.

Background check results travel directly from Checkr to our database via a cryptographically signed webhook. No human sees or touches them in transit. No admin can alter them. The result is permanent and unalterable — that's the point.

When you see a verified badge on Kyzmyt, it means something. It means the government confirmed that person's identity. It means a national criminal background check came back clean. It means the community you're in was built on a foundation that other platforms have never attempted.
