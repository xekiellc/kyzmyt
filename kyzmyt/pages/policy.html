Background Check Policy

We believe you have the right to know exactly what we check, what disqualifies someone, and how we handle records from years past. No vague promises. Just facts.

Last updated April 2026
What we check

Every Kyzmyt member must pass a real criminal background check before their profile goes live. This is not a self-reported form. Results go directly from our background check provider to us — the applicant never touches them.

  • ✓
    National criminal database — searches across all 50 states for criminal records
  • ✓
    Sex offender registry — checked against all 50 state registries and the national database
  • ✓
    Felony convictions — all felony convictions regardless of offense type
  • ✓
    Misdemeanor convictions — all misdemeanor convictions
  • ✓
    Pending charges — active criminal charges that have not yet been resolved

Results are delivered directly to Kyzmyt from our verified background check provider. The applicant cannot alter, hide, or selectively disclose what we receive.

What disqualifies an applicant

Not all records result in the same outcome. We use a category-based approach — the nature of the offense matters more than when it occurred.

Permanent Disqualifier
Sex offenses
Any sex offense — regardless of when it occurred, how long ago, or whether the applicant has served their time. No exceptions. No timeline.
Permanent Disqualifier
Violent felonies
Murder, manslaughter, assault, robbery, kidnapping, and other violent felony convictions. Permanent disqualifier regardless of timeline.
Case by Case Review
Non-violent older offenses
Non-violent felonies or misdemeanors from the distant past are reviewed individually. Context, timeline, and nature of the offense all factor in.
Automatic Flag
Pending charges
Active pending charges result in an automatic hold pending resolution. Applicants may reapply once charges are resolved.

Human review means a real Kyzmyt team member evaluates the specific circumstances — the nature of the offense, how long ago it occurred, and any other relevant context. We do not use automated decisions for case-by-case reviews.

How the process works
1
You apply for access
You submit your application through Kyzmyt. You provide your name, date of birth, and consent to a background check.
2
Identity is verified first
Before the background check runs, your identity is confirmed via government ID and live facial recognition match. This ensures the background check is run on you — not someone else's information.
3
Background check runs automatically
Our background check provider runs the check independently. Results are delivered directly to Kyzmyt — not through you. This typically takes 1–5 minutes.
4
Result is applied automatically
Clear results are auto-approved. Disqualifying results are auto-denied. Edge cases go to a human reviewer. You'll receive an email with your result.
5
Ongoing monitoring
Getting in doesn't mean staying in. We reserve the right to re-verify members and remove anyone whose status changes or whose behavior violates our standards.
Our commitment to transparency
Results go directly to us. Your background check results are delivered from our provider directly to Kyzmyt. You cannot see, alter, or selectively disclose them.

We don't share results with other members. Your background check result is used only to determine your eligibility. It is never shared with other members of the platform.

We don't sell your data. Your personal information and background check results are never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than verifying your eligibility for Kyzmyt.

No system is perfect. We have built more verification layers than any dating platform in existence. But we cannot guarantee that every bad actor will be caught. This policy exists to make our standards clear — not to make promises we cannot keep.
If you believe your result is wrong

Background check errors do occur. If you believe your result contains inaccurate information, you have the right to dispute it. Here's what to do:

  • 1
    Email us at hello@kyzmyt.com with the subject line "Background Check Dispute"
  • 2
    We will provide you with the name of our background check provider so you can dispute the record directly with them
  • 3
    Under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), you have the right to dispute inaccurate information in consumer reports
  • 4
    Once a dispute is resolved and your record is corrected, you may reapply to Kyzmyt
Important disclaimer
Kyzmyt's background check process is designed to reduce — not eliminate — the risk of harmful individuals joining the platform. No verification system is perfect. Background checks only reflect information that has been reported, prosecuted, and entered into public records. Kyzmyt makes no guarantee that all members are free of criminal history or that interactions on the platform are without risk. Members are encouraged to exercise their own judgment and report any concerning behavior immediately. By joining Kyzmyt, members acknowledge that they have read and understood this policy.
